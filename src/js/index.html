About

Plastic waste is named as one the biggest worldwide problems by a list of ecological organizations like Greenpeace and others. Of course, Cyprus couldn’t avoid it.

According to European Union Circular Economy Action Plan, there must be done a revision of Construction Product Regulation (link: ), including introduction of recycled content requirements for certain construction products. Moreover, the European Commission will propose mandatory requirements for recycled content for key products such as packaging, construction materials, etc.

SKRIF is a waste recycling production company. We transform plastic and glass waste into polymer-sand composition which can be colored and formed into different items by the order of our clients. Moreover, our products can be recycled itselves several times.

Our production offers exceptional resistance to a wide range of commonly encountered chemicals such as: wide range of mineral oils, fuels, battery acids, de-icing salts, alkaline cleaners, sea salt. Because of monolithic structure and total absence of pores, it resists not only high or low temperatures, but also temperature drop. It is also fouling and fungi resistant, which can be crucial for roof tiles for example.

With high mechanical strengths and a low elastic modulus, polymer-sand production is very resilient and able to withstand severe impact loads. While no material is indestructible and surface chipping may occur, cracking is unknown with our paving bricks or kerbstones. This set of factors prolongs lifetime of our products up to 50 years even under serious impact, thermal and chemical load.
